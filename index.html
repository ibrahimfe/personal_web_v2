Who Am I?

Hey, everyone! I'm Ibrahim, a computer science undergraduate student studying at Universitas Sultan Ageng Tirtayasa. I've always been fascinated by technology and how it can make our lives easier and more efficient. That's why I decided to pursue a degree in computer science. When I'm not working on my studies, you can find me tinkering with code, learning new programming languages, or playing video games.

I'm currently exploring various areas of computer science, including Web Development, Machine Learning, and Software Development. I'm also a member of Google Developer Student at Universitas Sultan Ageng Tirtayasa, where I've had the opportunity to collaborate with other students on exciting projects and attend talks from industry professionals. After graduation, I hope to pursue a career as aSoftware Developer and make a difference in the world through technology. Thanks for stopping by my page, and feel free to reach out to me if you'd like to connect!

During my studies, I've had the opportunity to work on a variety of projects that have helped me develop my skills and gain hands-on experience in the field. For example, I worked on a team to develop a Digital Library Program Using C++, The Purpose of the program is to digitalize library so that everyone can access it more freely. I also completed an internship at Internasional Office Universitas Sultan Ageng Tirtayasa where I manage and maintain IO Untirta to updating the content inside and uploading news about IO Untirta. These experiences have taught me the importance of collaboration, attention to detail, and problem-solving, and I'm excited to continue learning and growing in my future endeavors.
